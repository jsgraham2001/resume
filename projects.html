Bear Simulation

The Bear Simulation demonstrates a virtual ecosystem of various critters trying to survive the longest. Every creature or plant in the simulation is placed on a visible grid consisting of land and water. The simulation selects a thing at random and has it try and perform its various functions, which typically consist of trying to breed, trying to eat, and then trying to move. This process continues until either the life of the world ends, or either the total number of bears or smart Bears reaches 0. These two bears are the star of the show, with the regular bears choosing their movements randomly and the smart bears basing their moves on the distant presence of potential food. The simulation hopes to demonstrate the effectiveness of Artificial Intelligence and has the potential to incorporate other concepts such as reinforcement learning.
